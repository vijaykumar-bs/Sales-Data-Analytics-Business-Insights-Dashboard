## 10. Conclusion

The Olist marketplace shows **strong product-market fit** with healthy 21%+ YoY growth, a broad product catalog, and generally positive customer satisfaction (77% positive reviews). The key challenges are:

1. **Customer retention** — extremely low repeat purchase rates
2. **Geographic concentration** — SP accounts for 37% of revenue
3. **Delivery speed in remote areas** — directly impacts customer satisfaction

Addressing these three areas through targeted retention campaigns, regional logistics improvements, and seller ecosystem development represents the highest-ROI path for sustainable growth.
